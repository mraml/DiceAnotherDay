# Elven Valley Defense
## Solo Card Game Rules

---

## SETUP

1. Shuffle deck (52 cards + 2 Jokers)
2. Draw 3 cards, place vertically as walls: **Left | Center | Right**
   - Wall HP = card value (J=11, Q=12, K=13, A=14)
3. Draw 5 cards for your hand (max: 7)
4. Deal 6 face-down cards in 2x3 grid left of Left Wall (Left Army)
5. Deal 6 face-down cards in 2x3 grid right of Right Wall (Right Army)
   ```
   [Back: 2 cards] [Middle: 2 cards] [Front: 2 cards] → WALL
   ```
6. Place token on Center Wall
7. Empty space = your **Resource Chest**

---

## HOW TO PLAY

### TURN ORDER
1. **Your Turn** - Take 3 actions
2. **Left Army** - Flip 1 card, advance all
3. **Right Army** - Flip 1 card, advance all

### YOUR ACTIONS (Pick 3)

**MOVE** - Switch to Left/Center/Right Wall
- Wall gets +3 HP while you're there
- **DIE if:** Damage on your wall > (Wall HP + 3)

**ATTACK-RANGE (♠ Spades)** - Kill enemy ≤ value in Middle/Back rows → top of Chest

# Elven Valley Defense
## Solo Card Game Rules

---

## SETUP

1. Shuffle deck (52 cards + 2 Jokers)
2. Draw 3 cards, place vertically as walls: **Left | Center | Right**
   - Wall HP = card value (J=11, Q=12, K=13, A=14)
3. Draw 5 cards for your hand (max: 7)
4. **Draw 1 card for your starting Resource Chest**
5. Deal 6 face-down cards in 2x3 grid left of Left Wall (Left Army)
6. Deal 6 face-down cards in 2x3 grid right of Right Wall (Right Army)
   ```
   [Back: 2 cards] [Middle: 2 cards] [Front: 2 cards] → WALL
   ```
7. Place token on Center Wall cards in 2x3 grid right of Right Wall (Right Army)
   ```
   [Back: 2 cards] [Middle: 2 cards] [Front: 2 cards] → WALL
   ```
6. Place token on Center Wall
7. Empty space = your **Resource Chest**

---

## HOW TO PLAY

### TURN ORDER
1. **Your Turn** - Take 3 actions
2. **Left Army** - Flip 1 card, advance all
3. **Right Army** - Flip 1 card, advance all

### YOUR ACTIONS (Pick 3)

**MOVE** - Switch to Left/Center/Right Wall
- Wall gets +3 HP while you're there
- **DIE if:** Damage on your wall > (Wall HP + 3)

**ATTACK-RANGE (♠ Spades)** - Kill enemy ≤ value NOT at Wall or Front Row → top of Chest

**ATTACK-MELEE (♦ Diamonds)** - Kill enemy ≤ value at Wall or Front Row → top of Chest

**REINFORCE (♥ Hearts)** - Stand up broken wall with HP = card value

**TELEPORT (♣ Clubs)** - Move enemy ≤ value to back of their army

**DRAW** - Draw 2 cards
- **Joker drawn:** Swap any wall card with one from your hand (old wall → discard), discard Joker

**LOOT** - Use top Chest card immediately for its suit ability, then discard

### ARMY TURNS

**1. FLIP:** Reveal 1 face-down Back Row card
- **Face card (J/Q/K/A):** Draw 1 card from deck, add face-down behind army
- **Joker:** Destroys most recent naturally-flipped enemy, then discard Joker

**2. ADVANCE:** Cards move forward if space is empty, attacking the wall **closest to player** (max 1 wall away)
- **Player on Left:** Left Army → Left Wall, Right Army → Center Wall
- **Player on Center:** Both Armies → Center Wall
- **Player on Right:** Left Army → Center Wall, Right Army → Right Wall
- Movement: Front Row → Wall (adds damage), Middle → Front, Back → Middle

---

## WINNING & LOSING

**WIN:** Defeat all enemies, at least 1 wall standing

**LOSE:**
- Damage on your wall > Wall HP + 3
- All walls broken, can't reinforce
- Deck empty, can't act

---

## ENDLESS MODE

After winning a round:
1. Shuffle discard pile back into deck
2. Deal 2 new armies (6 cards each, 2x3 grids)
3. Keep your chest, walls, and hand
4. Continue playing - see how many waves you survive!

---

## QUICK REFERENCE

**Card Values:** 2-10 = face value, J=11, Q=12, K=13, A=14

**Suits:**
- ♠ = Range attack (not at Wall/Front Row)
- ♦ = Melee attack (at Wall/Front Row)
- ♥ = Rebuild wall
- ♣ = Teleport enemy back

**Joker (drawn):** Swap wall card with hand card

**Wall broken?** Damage ≥ Wall HP → turn sideways
